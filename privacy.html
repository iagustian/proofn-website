Privacy Policy

Last Updated: August 23, 2015

Proofn is a fast and fun way to send email with your friends, colleagues and contacts. Users can attach photos or documents to friends, send email that cannot be edited once sent (Non-Proofn email), send email that can be edited or deleted at anytime (Proofn email) and send email that can be edited or deleted before the intended recipient opens the email (Business Proofn email)

This privacy policy describes how we (Myabuy LLC, 360notion website, “Proofn” mobile application and our subsidiaries) collect, use, and share your information whenever you use our Services. Those Services include every way you might interact with us: through our websites, mobile applications, and any of our other online items and services that link to this privacy policy.

Edit, Delete or Remove Sent Email

Delete, edit or removing email you sent along with attachments is the default setting when sending Proofn email to “Clients/VIP” contacts. That means that Proofn email along with attachments can be changed and/or deleted anytime during your 90-day free trial. After the 90-day free trial, we will charge $2.99 per contact to change or delete Proofn email, Business Proofn email to “Client/VIP” contact(s) too.

But—and this is important—you should understand that users who see your email can always save them, either by taking a screenshot or by using some other image-capture technology (whether that be software or even something as old-fashioned as a camera to take a photo of your device’s screen). If we’re able to detect that a recipient took a screenshot of an email you sent, we’ll try to notify you. But the same common sense that applies to the Internet at large applies to Proofn as well: Don’t send email that you wouldn’t want someone to save or share.

You can mark up to five (5) contacts as “Client/VIP” and send Business Proofn email along with attachments that can be changed and/or deleted anytime before the recipient(s) open your Business Proofn email. For every subsequent contact you mark as “Client/VIP” we will charge $2.99 per contact.

You can also choose to send Non-Proofn email along with attachments that cannot be edited and deleted or removed later.

A few final words on editing, deletion and removals on Proofn email sent to “Clients/VIP” contacts: We can’t guarantee that email will be deleted or attachments removed within a specific timeframe. And even after we’ve deleted email data or attachments from our servers, that same data or attachment may remain in backup for a limited period of time. We also sometimes receive requests from law enforcement requiring us by law to suspend our ordinary server-deletion practices for specific information. Finally, of course, as with any digital information, there may be ways to access email while still in temporary storage on recipients’ devices or, forensically, even after they are deleted.

Non-Proofn Email

You can always send Non-Proofn email at anytime before or after your 90-day free trial ends. Non-Proofn email means sending email and attachments that you cannot edit, delete or remove later.

You can mark 50 emails in your inbox that you want us to automatically delete on your behalf in 24 hours. When you mark over 50 emails in your inbox, we require a $.3.60 payment to mark an additional 50 emails in your inbox that you want us to automatically delete on your behalf in 24 hours.
